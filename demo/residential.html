Residential Services We Offer

Wiring & Rewiring

New wiring for additions, knob-and-tube replacement, and full home rewires. Code-compliant, safe, and built to last.

Panel Upgrades

Upgrade from 100A to 200A (or higher) to support modern appliances, EV chargers, and whole-home generators.

Lighting & Fixtures

Recessed LED lighting, chandeliers, dimmer switches, under-cabinet lighting, and landscape lighting design.

EV Charger Installation

Level 2 charger installation for Tesla, J1772, and all EV makes. Dedicated 50A circuits with surge protection.

Generator Installation

Generac and Kohler standby generators with automatic transfer switches. Keep your home running during outages.

Smart Home Automation

Lighting controls, motorized shades, thermostats, doorbell cams, and whole-home audio wiring.
